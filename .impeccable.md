## Design Context

### Users

SitePitch is for German-speaking freelance web designers and small agencies that prospect manually. They use a fast website audit to turn a weak, generic cold message into a concrete conversation starter and a professional, shareable sales asset.

### Brand Personality

Verkaufsstark, direkt, konkret. The product should create confidence and momentum without hype, fabricated proof, compliance promises, or language that embarrasses the audited business.

### Aesthetic Direction

For TASK-4.15, build a light, product-led structural scaffold from shadcnblocks and the existing SitePitch indigo/OKLCH tokens. Prioritize real report excerpts, clear hierarchy, and visible conversion paths. Detailed visual styling, distinctive typography, motion, and decorative art direction are intentionally deferred to the next design pass.

### Design Principles

- Lead with the customer outcome: better sales conversations, not technical audit machinery.
- Show concrete report evidence before asking visitors to trust claims.
- Keep one dominant action per section and make demo, signup, and billing handoffs explicit.
- Use honest MVP capabilities and pricing; no fake logos, testimonials, speed claims, or guarantees.
- Preserve WCAG AA basics, keyboard completion, mobile usability, reduced motion, and readable line lengths from the first scaffold.
