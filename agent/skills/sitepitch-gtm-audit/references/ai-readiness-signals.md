# AI Readiness Signals

Use these as optional signals, not guarantees.

Helpful signals:
- clear title and meta description
- clear H1 and service description
- crawlable sitemap
- robots.txt that does not block important pages
- structured data / schema types
- clear contact and business information
- readable, specific page copy

llms.txt:
- Treat as optional and emerging.
- Do not mark missing llms.txt as a mandatory failure.
- Do not claim that llms.txt guarantees AI citations.
