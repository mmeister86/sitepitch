# GTM Opportunity Matrix

Use this mapping when turning evidence into GTM opportunities.

| Evidence Pattern | Opportunity |
|---|---|
| CTA or contact path is unclear | Conversion opportunity |
| Title, meta, H1, or service clarity is weak | SEO and conversion opportunity |
| Schema, sitemap, robots, or structured content issues | Discoverability opportunity |
| Trust, contact, proof, or credibility gaps | Trust and outreach opportunity |
| Persona friction from persona-review | Concrete next-step opportunity |
| Design or hierarchy issue from critique | Prioritization and visual clarity opportunity |
